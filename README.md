## 🎯 Executive Summary

This repository presents a structured Data Analytics portfolio built using Power BI, demonstrating end-to-end analytical capability across two business domains:

## 📈 Key Business Insights

- Data Engineering and Data Analysis roles dominate job market demand.
- Median Data Science salary is approximately **$125K**, indicating strong compensation stability.
- Hiring activity shows a noticeable slowdown post mid-2024.
- Remote work listings remain limited (~14%), indicating hybrid shift.
- Prime Video content is heavily skewed toward TV Shows (81%).
- Drama is the most dominant genre, indicating high saturation.
- Content growth peaked around 2021 during global streaming expansion.
- Geographic content distribution is concentrated in North America and India.
