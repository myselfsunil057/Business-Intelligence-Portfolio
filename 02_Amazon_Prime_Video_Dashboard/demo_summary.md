# 🎬 Amazon Prime Video Dashboard  

---

## 📌 Description

The **Amazon Prime Video Content Analysis Dashboard** is a business intelligence solution that evaluates the platform’s content library to understand distribution patterns, audience targeting, and content strategy effectiveness.

It analyzes metadata across thousands of titles to uncover insights related to content type distribution, genre dominance, geographic representation, and audience rating segmentation.

---

## 🧠 Key Focus Areas
- Movies vs TV Shows distribution
- Genre popularity and dominance analysis
- Content growth trends over time
- Geographic distribution of content production
- Rating-based audience segmentation

---

## 📊 Business Value
- Identifies content saturation areas (e.g., over-reliance on Drama)
- Highlights underrepresented genres and regions
- Supports strategic content acquisition planning
- Helps evaluate platform audience targeting strategy

---

## 🎯 Outcome

A strategic content intelligence system that reveals how streaming platforms structure and optimize their content libraries for engagement and growth.
