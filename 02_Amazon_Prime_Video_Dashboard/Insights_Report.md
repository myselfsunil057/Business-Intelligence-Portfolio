# 🎬 Amazon Prime Video Dashboard 

## 🎞️ Content Composition Analysis

### 📌 Key Findings
- TV Shows: **81%**
- Movies: **19%**

### 🧠 Interpretation
The platform strategy clearly prioritizes:
- Long-form engagement (TV shows > movies)

### 📊 Business Impact
- Indicates a **subscription retention-driven strategy**
- Focus on continuous user engagement rather than one-time consumption

---

## 🎭 Genre Distribution

### 📌 Key Findings
- Drama is the dominant genre (highest volume)
- Comedy is the second major category
- Other genres have significantly lower representation

### 🧠 Interpretation
The content library is:
- Emotion-driven
- Heavily dependent on mainstream genres

### 📊 Business Impact
- Risk of **genre saturation**, especially in Drama
- Strong opportunity for **niche and diversified content expansion**

---

## 📈 Content Growth Trends

### 📌 Key Findings
- Minimal content available before 1990
- Strong growth observed after 2010
- Peak expansion occurred around **2021**

### 🧠 Interpretation
Growth aligns with:
- Global streaming adoption wave
- OTT platform expansion era

### 📊 Business Impact
- Content expansion is **wave-driven, not linear**
- Suggests **reactive content acquisition strategy** influenced by market competition

---

## 🌍 Geographic Distribution

### 📌 Key Findings
- High concentration: **North America, India**
- Low representation: **Africa, South America**

### 🧠 Interpretation
The content library shows:
- Clear regional imbalance
- Strong market-driven content sourcing bias

### 📊 Business Impact
- Significant opportunity for **regional expansion**
- Strong need for **localization and global content diversification strategy**

---

## 👶 Content Rating Distribution

### 📌 Key Findings
- Majority content rated **13+ and 16+**
- Significant portion of **18+ rated content**

### 🧠 Interpretation
Target audience is primarily:
- Teen to adult demographic
- Limited focus on family-friendly content

### 📊 Business Impact
- Platform positioning is **mature-audience oriented**
- Restricts penetration into **children/family content segment**
