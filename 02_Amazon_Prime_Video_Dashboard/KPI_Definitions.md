## 🎞️ Content Share (Movies vs TV Shows)

### 📌 Definition
Measures the proportional distribution of Movies and TV Shows in the content library.

### 🧮 Formula Concept
Content Share = (Category Count / Total Content) × 100

### 📊 Business Relevance
- Identifies platform content strategy orientation
- Measures focus on episodic vs standalone content

---

## 🎭 enre Dominance Index

### 📌 Definition
A ranking-based KPI that measures how frequently each genre appears in the dataset.

### 🧮 Formula Concept
Genre Frequency Count → Ranked descending

### 📊 Business Relevance
- Identifies overrepresented genres
- Helps detect content saturation risks
- Supports diversification strategy

---

## 📈 Content Growth Rate

### 📌 Definition
Measures year-over-year increase in content additions.

### 🧮 Formula Concept
Growth Rate = (Current Year Content - Previous Year Content) / Previous Year Content × 100

### 📊 Business Relevance
- Tracks platform expansion speed
- Identifies content acquisition cycles
- Measures OTT growth phases

---

## 🌍 Geographic Density Score

### 📌 Definition
Measures content concentration across countries/regions.

### 🧮 Formula Concept
Geographic Density = COUNT(Content Titles) grouped by Country

### 📊 Business Relevance
- Identifies regional content imbalance
- Supports localization strategy planning
- Highlights underrepresented markets

---

## 👶 Rating Distribution Ratio

### 📌 Definition
Distribution of content across age rating categories (13+, 16+, 18+, etc.)

### 🧮 Formula Concept
Rating Ratio = (Content in Rating Category / Total Content) × 100

### 📊 Business Relevance
- Identifies target audience segmentation
- Measures platform content maturity level
- Supports content compliance strategy
