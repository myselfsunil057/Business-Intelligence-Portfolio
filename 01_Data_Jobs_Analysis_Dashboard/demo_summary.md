# 📊 Data Jobs Analysis Dashboard  

---

## 📌 Description

The **Data Jobs Analysis Dashboard** is a business intelligence system designed to analyze global data job market trends using large-scale job posting data. It focuses on understanding salary structures, demand distribution, hiring patterns, and workforce flexibility across the data industry.

This dashboard transforms raw job listing data into structured insights that support career planning, skill prioritization, and labor market evaluation.

---

## 🧠 Key Focus Areas
- Salary benchmarking across data-related roles
- Job demand distribution (Data Analyst, Data Engineer, Data Scientist, etc.)
- Hiring trend analysis over time
- Remote vs onsite work distribution
- Degree requirement analysis in job postings

---

## 📊 Business Value
- Helps professionals identify high-demand career paths
- Supports salary expectation benchmarking
- Reveals hiring cycle patterns and market slowdowns
- Highlights industry shift toward infrastructure-heavy roles

---

## 🎯 Outcome

A structured view of the global data job market that enables **data-driven career and recruitment decisions**.
