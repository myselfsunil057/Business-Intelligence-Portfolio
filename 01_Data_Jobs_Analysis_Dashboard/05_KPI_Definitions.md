---

## 💰 Median Salary

### 📌 Definition
The median salary represents the **central value of all salary observations**, where 50% of job listings fall below and 50% fall above.

### 🧮 Formula Concept
  - Median = Middle value of sorted salary distribution

### 📊 Business Relevance
  - Reduces impact of extreme outliers (e.g., executive-level salaries)
  - Provides realistic salary expectations for candidates

---

## 📈 Job Demand Count

### 📌 Definition
Total number of job postings grouped by role category.

### 🧮 Formula Concept
Job Demand = COUNT(Job_ID) grouped by Job_Title_Category

### 📊 Business Relevance
- Identifies high-demand job roles
- Helps prioritize skill development paths
- Indicates market saturation levels

---

## 📉 Hiring Trend Index

### 📌 Definition
A time-based measure tracking the **volume of job postings over time**.

### 🧮 Formula Concept
Hiring Trend Index = COUNT(Job_Postings) grouped by time period (month/year)

### 📊 Business Relevance
- Detects hiring slowdowns and growth cycles
- Useful for macro-level labor market forecasting
- Identifies economic or industry shifts

---

## 🏡 Remote Work Ratio

### 📌 Definition
Percentage of job postings that allow remote work.

### 🧮 Formula Concept
Remote Work Ratio = (Remote Jobs / Total Jobs) × 100

### 📊 Business Relevance
- Measures workplace flexibility trends
- Indicates adoption of remote-first policies
- Helps job seekers target flexible roles

---

## 🎓 Degree Requirement Ratio

### 📌 Definition
Percentage of job postings requiring formal education (degree requirement specified).

### 🧮 Formula Concept
Degree Ratio = (Jobs requiring degree / Total Jobs) × 100

### 📊 Business Relevance
- Indicates barrier to entry in the job market
- Measures shift toward skill-based hiring vs credential-based hiring
